## What is VizBlitz?

VizBlitz is a powerful visioning session designed for founders and companies at a crossroads -- whether you're pivoting, scaling, or trying to figure out what's next. In 60 focused minutes, Susan helps participants bring future possibilities into sharp focus and build a practical tool to stay on track.

> *"VizBlitz anchored our vision in a bigger, more evocative way -- it is powerful."*
> -- B. Geuy, Founder & CEO, PODS

## Who Is It For?

VizBlitz is perfect for:

- **Founders facing pivotal decisions** about direction or growth
- **Companies in transition** navigating market shifts or leadership changes
- **Teams** that need to align on a shared vision for the future
- **Individuals** at a career or life crossroads seeking clarity

## How It Works

Susan guides participants through a structured visioning process that draws on her four decades of experience helping people navigate uncertainty. The session moves from current reality through possibility exploration to practical grounding.

The result is not just a dream -- it's a vision anchored in actionable steps.

## What You'll Walk Away With

- A vivid, articulated vision of your desired future
- A practical framework for tracking progress toward that vision
- Clarity on what matters most and what to let go of
- Renewed energy and confidence for the road ahead
